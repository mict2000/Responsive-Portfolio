About Me

I have lived most of my life in Southern California and Hawaii. Not being within walking distance to the surf is a culture shock for me. However, I have become fascinated with the technology world in which I have found myself immersed and I intend to become a Full Stack Developer as soon as possible. When in Rome...
Soon, I intend to become a professional in the field and combine my passions to make the world an even smaller place by connecting with people through surfing, music, and a love of meeting new people and traveling off the grid. I believe people only fear what they do not know and this is what causes strife. Once we connect cultures, peace thrives. I believe we are the generation that will begin to make war obsolete and our children will complete the mission.
